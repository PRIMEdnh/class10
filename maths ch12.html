Very Short Answer Questions (VSA)

  1. Define lateral surface area of a cylinder.
    Answer Lateral surface area (CSA) = 2πrh
  2. What is the formula for the total surface area of a cube?
    Answer TSA = 6a²
  3. Volume of a cube of side a is?
    Answer V = a³
  4. Define the volume of a sphere.
    Answer V = (4/3)πr³
  5. What is the surface area of a hemisphere?
    Answer TSA = 3πr²
  6. Volume of a cylinder of radius r and height h?
    Answer V = πr²h
  7. Curved surface area of a cone with radius r and slant height l?
    Answer CSA = πrl
  8. Total surface area of a cone?
    Answer TSA = πr(l + r)
  9. Define a right circular cone.
    Answer A cone whose apex is above the center of base circle.
  10. Relationship between diameter and radius of a sphere.
    Answer Diameter = 2 × radius
  11. Surface area of a cube with side 5 cm.
    Answer TSA = 6 × 5² = 150 cm²
  12. Volume of a cube with side 4 cm.
    Answer V = 4³ = 64 cm³
  13. Curved surface area of cylinder radius 3 cm height 7 cm.
    Answer CSA = 2πrh = 2×3.14×3×7 ≈ 131.88 cm²
  14. Total surface area of a hemisphere radius 7 cm.
    Answer TSA = 3πr² = 3×3.14×49 ≈ 461.58 cm²
  15. Volume of cone radius 3 cm height 4 cm.
    Answer V = (1/3)πr²h = (1/3)×3.14×9×4 ≈ 37.68 cm³
