Very Short Answer Questions

  1. Define a triangle.
    AnswerA polygon with three sides and three angles.
  2. What is an equilateral triangle?
    AnswerA triangle with all sides equal.
  3. What is an isosceles triangle?
    AnswerA triangle with two sides equal.
  4. What is a scalene triangle?
    AnswerA triangle with all sides different.
  5. What is a right triangle?
    AnswerA triangle with one 90¬∞ angle.
  6. Define median of a triangle.
    AnswerA line joining a vertex to the midpoint of the opposite side.
  7. Define altitude of a triangle.
    AnswerA perpendicular from a vertex to the opposite side.
  8. Define angle bisector of a triangle.
    AnswerA line dividing an angle into two equal angles.
  9. When are two triangles similar?
    AnswerWhen their corresponding angles are equal and sides are proportional.
  10. What is the centroid of a triangle?
    AnswerThe point of intersection of medians.
  11. What is the orthocenter of a triangle?
    AnswerThe point of intersection of altitudes.
  12. What is the incenter of a triangle?
    AnswerThe point of intersection of angle bisectors.
  13. What is the circumcenter of a triangle?
    AnswerThe point of intersection of perpendicular bisectors of sides.
  14. Write one property of equilateral triangle.
    AnswerAll angles are 60¬∞.
  15. Write one property of isosceles triangle.
    AnswerAngles opposite equal sides are equal.
